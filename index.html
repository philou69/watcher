CastWatcher

For chromecasting local video that navigator don't read.

Why use us

The chromecast device is a awesome device.
But when you want to cast an video store on you device, you can only use some files ( mp4,...). With CastWatcher, you can play a large choices of video's type (mkv, ....). And you can adding subtitle. CastWatcher read your video on local, we don't download the file.
CastWatcher is open source, you can find use on github
